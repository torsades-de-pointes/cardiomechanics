Normal physiology — baseline

ESPVR is drawn through the actual end-systolic point (③ AV Closes) using a back-calculated V₀_eff so the line is guaranteed to pass through the simulation-derived point in all pathologies. Ea = ESP ÷ SV, drawn from (EDV, 0) → (ESV, ESP). Optimal coupling: Ea/Ees ≈ 0.6–1.2.

Ao SBP / DBP are aortic pressures from the Windkessel model — not peak LVP. In Aortic Stenosis, peak LVP >> Ao SBP (transvalvular gradient visible). Toggle "Peak LVP line" to see this.

PVA potential energy = region between ESPVR (top), IVR vertical at ESV (right), and EDPVR curve (bottom). Proportional to myocardial O₂ consumption (Suga 1981).
